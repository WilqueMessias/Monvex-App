Monvex Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains, in plain language, what data Monvex uses to work, what stays on your device, and what is sent to external services for exchange-rate lookup.

Last updated: July 2026 App version: internal-test-1 Platform: Android

1. Introduction

Monvex is a currency converter and calculator prepared for Android and Google Play. This page describes how data is handled while you use the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data We Do Not Collect

Monvex does not collect the following personal or sensitive data:

  • Name or personal details
  • Email address or phone number
  • Exact or approximate location
  • Photos, videos, or files
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Browsing history
  • Biometric data

2.2 Data Implicitly Used in Network Requests

When fetching live exchange rates, the app makes HTTPS requests to third-party APIs. These requests may include your device IP address and User-Agent.

This data is sent directly to the API provider and is not stored by Monvex.

2.3 Local Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Conversion history
  • Language preferences
  • Subscription status, if applicable

This data remains exclusively on your device and is not sent to Monvex servers.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide currency conversion functionality
  • Display updated exchange rates
  • Keep your local conversion history
  • Improve app compatibility with your device

4. Data Sharing

Sharing summary
  • We do not sell or share personal data.
  • IP address and User-Agent may be shared only with exchange-rate API providers to make the app work.
  • We do not integrate analytics or third-party tracking libraries for personal data collection.

5. Security

  • All API requests use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Local data is stored securely on your device.
  • We do not keep copies of user data on Monvex servers.

6. Data Deletion

Local history: you can remove it by:

  • Using the clear button inside the app
  • Opening Android Settings → Apps → Monvex → Storage → Clear cache
  • Uninstalling the app

The API provider may retain logs according to its own policy. Monvex does not keep backups of user data.

7. User Rights

  • Access local data at any time
  • Clear history inside the app
  • Uninstall the app at any time
  • Not be tracked by third parties through Monvex

8. Legal Compliance

This policy is written to be compatible with:

  • GDPR
  • COPPA
  • LGPD
